## 3 · Reel — 12 seconds

**Visual:** screen recording —
1. drag the globe
2. click a country
3. dossier slides in, scroll the entries
4. click "+ post yours"
5. type a concern, submit
6. red ping pulses at the country

**On-screen text (sequenced):**

- 0–2s: "what is your concern, right now?"
- 3–6s: "anonymous · global · permanent"
- 7–10s: (show post → ping)
- 11–12s: "whatisyourconcern.com"

**Caption:**

> drop a sentence. somebody, somewhere, will read it.
> whatisyourconcern.com

**Audio:** something soft + slightly unsettling — Lord Huron "The Night We Met", Burial, or the muted hum of *Children of Men*.

## 4 · Story sequence (5 stories, post over 2–3 days)

1. Plain text on ink: *"a quiet room. no accounts. no names."* — link sticker.
2. Screenshot of three real concerns from different countries — link sticker.
3. Question sticker: *"what is your concern, right now?"* — let people answer in stories.
4. Repost a couple of the answers (anonymized) with *"there's a place for this →"* + link.
5. Globe screenshot, caption *"30 countries so far"* + link.

---

## 5 · DM script — send to ~20 close friends first

> hey — built something. it's an anonymous global record of what people are afraid of, mapped on a globe. no accounts, no names. would mean a lot if you'd post one thing — even one sentence — so it has voices when I share it more widely. whatisyourconcern.com

Do this BEFORE the public posts. The first 50 voices should come from people you trust to write something real, otherwise the seeds carry it alone and it feels staged.

## Order of operations

1. Wire Supabase + Turnstile env vars in Vercel (see `README.md`).
2. DM script → close friends. Let real submissions arrive for 24–48h.
3. Story sequence → warms the audience.
4. Carousel post → biggest reach moment.
5. Reel a day or two later → rides the algorithm.
6. Single-image post when traffic feels stale.

Don't post all five in one day. Space them.
