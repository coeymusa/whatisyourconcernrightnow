# TikTok — concepts for @whatisyourconcern

TikTok rewards two things: a **hook in the first 3 seconds** and **muted-readability** (most users watch with sound off, so on-screen text matters more than voiceover).

Your site has unfair advantages here — the spinning globe is visually arresting, and the concerns themselves are an emotional hook before any caption is written. Don't fight TikTok's tone with corporate copy. Lean into the editorial-dystopia look.

Format basics:
- **9:16 vertical**, recorded at 60fps if your phone allows
- 15–30s sweet spot for first videos (algorithm favours rewatch ratio)
- ≤4 hashtags. More looks spammy.
- Caption = the hook. The on-screen text = the hook again, bigger.

---

## Concept 1 — "voices from around the world" (start with this one)

**The simplest, most replicable, and most likely to go viral.**

### What to record (~22s)

| Time | Visual | On-screen text |
|---|---|---|
| 0–3s | Globe with one bubble already popped (Ukraine voice) | `what is the world afraid of?` |
| 3–7s | Drag globe to Brazil, click a dot, dossier slides in | `click any country` |
| 7–12s | Scroll the country list, show 2–3 concerns | `read what people are saying` |
| 12–17s | Open post form, type "the heat is getting personal", submit, dot pings | `add yours. anonymously.` |
| 17–22s | Pull back to globe, pings/voices visible | `whatisyourconcern.com` |

### Caption

> 1k voices from 25 countries. drag the globe and read what people are scared of, anonymously. add yours.

### Sound

Use a **trending melancholic / atmospheric sound**. Don't overthink. Open TikTok → tap "+" → tap the music note → search "sad piano" or "ambient" → pick one with 5k–500k uses (high enough to catch the algo, low enough to not be saturated).

Specific picks that fit the tone right now (check they're still trending when you post):
- *"experience"* by Ludovico Einaudi (cinematic, mournful)
- *"clair de lune"* lofi remix (always trending in waves)
- Any *"the night we met"* x Lord Huron clip

Avoid pop / hype tracks. They fight the tone of the site.

### Hashtags

```
#anonymous #internetisbeautiful #onlineculture #sadtok
```

## Concept 2 — "POV: you've been holding this in for years"

POV format is dominant on TikTok and works perfectly for the site.

### What to record (~12s)

1. (0s) Lock screen of phone → unlock to whatisyourconcern.com
2. (3s) Type a *real, vulnerable* concern in the post box. Don't fake one. Use something you've actually felt.
3. (8s) Submit. The "✓ recorded" toast.
4. (10s) Pan to the globe — the dot pings.
5. (12s) Cut to black with text: `whatisyourconcern.com`

**On-screen text** (full duration): `POV: you've been holding this in for years` — fades to `now there's a place for it`.

### Caption

> i don't know who needed to hear this but the site has no accounts. nobody knows it's you.

### Hashtags

```
#anxiety #pov #vent
```

## Concept 3 — "one fear from every continent"

Slideshow style. Pure visual. Strong for shares.

### What to record (~25s)

7 cards, each ~3s:

1. NORTH AMERICA — `"I won't be able to afford a child even if I wanted one." — age 28, USA`
2. SOUTH AMERICA — `"the rainforest is being sold and we are watching live." — age 30, Brazil`
3. EUROPE — `"my son is 18 next year. I can't breathe." — age 42, Ukraine`
4. AFRICA — `"japa. we are all leaving. the country is hollowing out." — age 22, Nigeria`
5. ASIA — `"loneliness is killing more of us than any disease." — age 41, Japan`
6. OCEANIA — `"another summer of red skies. we keep voting like it's normal." — age 38, Australia`
7. ANTARCTICA — black slide: `"this is everywhere. → whatisyourconcern.com"`

Each slide: italic serif on ink, slow ken-burns zoom.

### Caption

> 7 voices, 7 continents, all anonymous. there are 1k more on whatisyourconcern.com.

### Sound

Same as Concept 1 — atmospheric / cinematic.

### Hashtags

```
#worldnews #climateanxiety #mentalhealth
```

## Concept 4 — "watch the dots arrive"

A patient, voyeuristic angle. Less viral but high *rewatch rate*, which TikTok loves.

### What to record (~30s, no edits)

Set the globe rotating slowly. Don't touch it. As bubbles pop up, screen-record a full 30s of them arriving. Slow zoom-in on the globe over the duration.

### On-screen text

(0–3s): `every dot is a person.`
(7–10s): `every voice is anonymous.`
(20–25s): `whatisyourconcern.com`

### Caption

> sometimes I just let it run. these are real, anonymous, from people in 25 countries.

### Sound

Long ambient track — *Burial*, or any TikTok ambient meditation sound that runs > 30s.

### Hashtags

```
#liminalspace #ambient #internetisbeautiful
```

## Posting cadence

- First video: **Concept 1**. Hits hook + interactivity + CTA in 22 seconds.
- 24h later: **Concept 2** (POV). Different audience, complements first.
- 48h later: **Concept 3** (continents). Most shareable.
- 4–5 days in: **Concept 4** (ambient). For followers who already understand the site.

Don't post all four in one day. Algo wants you spaced out — gives each video a fair shot.

## TikTok-specific tips for a 0-follower account

1. **Reply to comments inside the first 2 hours.** Comment-engagement is the single biggest signal in TikTok's algo. Don't post and ghost.
2. **Pin the best one to your profile** — first 3 grid slots are what new visitors see.
3. **Use the trending sound.** Even if you have voiceover, tap "Volume" → set original audio to ~40%, trending sound to ~60%. The sound itself helps surface you in the For You feed.
4. **Don't put the link in the video.** Put `whatisyourconcern.com` in your **bio** + on-screen at the very end. TikTok suppresses videos that say "click the link" too aggressively.
5. **Recycle**. If a video gets 5k+ views, repost a near-identical version 2–3 weeks later with a different first frame. Different cohort, same hook.
