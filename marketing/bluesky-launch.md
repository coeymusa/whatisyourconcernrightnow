## Bluesky-specific tips

- **Starter packs** are how new accounts get followers fast. Search `indie hackers` / `tech` / `internet weirdness` / `data viz` — join a few in one click. Way more efficient than hand-picking like on Twitter.
- **Reply more than you post** for the first 2 weeks. The follow culture is "I saw them in a thread, they were thoughtful, I followed."
- **Custom feeds** are weighted higher than home feed. Engage with posts in `the-algorithm`, `tech-journalists`, `wow`, `art` and you'll surface to people who weren't going to search for you.
- **No link suppression** — share `coreyscodecave.com` and `whatisyourconcern.com` freely. Bluesky doesn't down-rank links the way X and IG do.
