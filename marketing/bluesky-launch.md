## Things Bluesky-specific worth knowing

- **Starter packs** are huge on Bluesky. Search `tech` / `indie hackers` / `data viz` — find a few starter packs, follow them in one click, follow ~30 people that way. Way faster than hand-picking individuals like on Twitter.
- **Custom feeds** matter more than the home feed. Find feeds for `the-algorithm`, `tech-journalists`, `art`, `wow` — engage with posts there to surface yourself.
- **Zero algorithmic suppression of links** — sharing whatisyourconcern.com doesn't get throttled the way it does on X. Post the link freely.
- **Engage in replies more than your own posts** for the first 2 weeks. The follow culture is "saw them in a thread, followed".
