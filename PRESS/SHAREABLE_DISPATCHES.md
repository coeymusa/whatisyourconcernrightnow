## How to use

When you wake up:

1. **Pick one** that matches the platform you're posting to (notes above).
2. **Open the permalink in a browser** and paste it into X/Bluesky/wherever — the OG card renders the quote on a poster automatically.
3. **Don't add your own commentary above the URL.** Let the quote land alone. If you want to say something, post a reply on your own post afterward — gives the algorithm a second engagement signal.
4. **The post should be just the URL on its own line.** No `🧵`, no "interesting project I built", no link-in-bio.

If a post starts taking off (>50 reposts in 30 min), follow up:
- Reply to your own post with a second permalink from this list. Different country, different topic.
- Pin the post for 24h.
- The third reply should link to the homepage (`whatisyourconcern.com`) — that's where new submissions actually happen.
