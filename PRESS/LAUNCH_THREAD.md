# Launch thread (X / Bluesky)

## Headline tweet (the one people see in their feed)

```
i made a place on the internet where you post one fear,
anonymously, and it goes on a 3d globe of the earth at
the country it came from.

it cannot be edited. it cannot be deleted. one voice,
one entry.

whatisyourconcern.com
```

Why this opens well: short, no jargon, the hook is in the first line.
Don't open with "excited to announce" — it kills 70% of impressions.

## Bluesky-specific tweaks

The Bluesky audience is a bit more receptive to longer, more
personal opening posts. Optionally lengthen the headline to:

```
i made a place where anyone can post one fear,
anonymously. it goes on a 3d globe at the country
it came from. no accounts, no edits, no deletions.

a quiet room, owned by nobody, where the species
can speak to itself.

whatisyourconcern.com
```

## Things to do before posting

1. Make sure your X / Bluesky profile clearly attributes the project
   (one line in bio, link in pinned post).
2. Confirm the OG card renders right by pasting the URL into
   https://x.com/intent/tweet?text=test&url=https://whatisyourconcern.com
   and previewing.
3. Have 2-3 people who will reply with their own concern in the
   first hour — replies are how Twitter and Bluesky decide your
   post deserves wider distribution.

## Time

Sunday night 7-9pm or Tuesday morning 9-11am, in whatever timezone
covers the most of your audience. Avoid news-cycle dominators
(big election day, big tech-layoff day, etc).

## Don't

- Don't use hashtags. They cap distribution on both platforms.
- Don't add a "founder" bio line.  the work should be the work.
- Don't quote-tweet your own post in 24h. It looks needy.
